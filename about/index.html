About - Read & Recommend

Hi, I'm Josh

Welcome to Read & Recommend! I'm a reader and a writer. I also like building websites. This is where these three hobbies come together. Read and Recommend is a place for me to share the notes and thoughts I've gathered on the books I've read, practice the craft of writing, and hone the skill of developing a website. I've intentionally chosen a retro 90's theme for this site because the internet is full of garbage. Fast Next.js sites with flashy graphics. I wanted to create something that is undeniably human. Something that predates the rapidly necrotizing dead internet.

What You'll Find Here

I write honest recommendations about the books I read. I have ADD and as a result read a few books at once and they are always drastically different from each other. This means my genres of choice change with the wind. I am a writer and work on multiple projects at once and they vary in genre and subject as well. It's usually the rabbit hole of the research for those books that leads me into genres. I wouldn't try to make sense of my genre choices. The answer is all of them.

My Reading Philosophy

I believe in:

  • Honest takes - I share what I genuinely think, not what I think people want to hear
  • Diverse reading - Fiction, non-fiction, classics, contemporary - I read it all
  • Reader recommendations - If you've got a book I should check out, let me know!
